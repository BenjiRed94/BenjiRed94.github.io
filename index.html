Who am I

My name is Ben. I'm a 25 year old, self-taught enthusiast of Web Design.

I take great enjoyment of learning new things, and the Web Design industry is constantly in flux, with new technologies cropping up near every day - unending learning possibilities are out there, and I'm glad to be sinking my teeth into them.

I first starting learning Web Design back in 2013, through WYSIWYG (What You See Is What You Get) - but my interest in it bloomed in late 2016, where I began reading articles and blogs about web design, and started creating some of my own small designs based on what I'd learnt.
